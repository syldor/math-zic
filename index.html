About the music Gaussian Curve is an ambient music trio composed by Young Marco, Gigi Masin and Jonny Nash. Gigi Masin is a long time ambient music producer and after the release of a compilation of his work, the two electronic music producers Young Marco and Jonny Nash started to work with him on the Gaussian Curve project and released Clouds, first album full of beauty.
